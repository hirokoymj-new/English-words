<h1>disrupt</h1>

- 崩壊させる、粉砕する、破裂させる、(一時的に)混乱させる
- disrupted; disrupting; disrupts
- **Synonyms**: break, break up, bust, fracture
- The barking dogs disrupted my sleep.
- The weather disrupted our travel plans.
